Club Goals

  1. Inclusiveness: No weeaboo shall be left behind. All members are welcome to express their passions. We are a safe place, free from judgement.
  2. Appreciation: We strive to apprecite and explore a variety of Japnaese media.
  3. Community: We are a community of friends who want to share our passions amongst ourselves, and others.

Who are we?

Weeb Nation is a community group at the University of Washington of students who enjoy watching anime, reading manga, attending conventions, and cosplaying.
